About Thermo Engineers

Established in 1998, Thermo Engineers is a trusted manufacturer of EPS thermocol plant machinery, hydraulic components, and custom industrial solutions based in Greater Noida, India. Our advanced facility is equipped for the complete in-house design, fabrication, and testing of machinery used in packaging, insulation, recycling, warehousing, and logistics.

Core Offerings

  • EPS Thermocol Machinery

    Pre-expanders, shape mold machines, block molds, and cutting machines optimized for high-volume and energy-efficient EPS production.

  • Bale Press Systems

    Manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic balers designed for compacting PET bottles and recyclable materials.

  • Material Handling Equipment

    Hydraulic stackers, scissor lifts, floor cranes, hoists, and goods lifts for efficient logistics and heavy load management.
